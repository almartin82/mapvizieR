# school growth norms 2012 study

school / cohort growth norms published by NWEA these are scraped out of
the published PDF tables

## Usage

``` r
sch_growth_norms_2012

sch_growth_norms_2012
```

- end_grade:

  measurementscale

  end_fallwinterspring
