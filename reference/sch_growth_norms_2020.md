# school growth norms 2020 study

School / cohort conditional growth norms published by NWEA. These are
parsed from Appendix E of the 2020 MAP Growth Normative Data report.
Growth expectations are conditional on starting RIT score.

## Usage

``` r
sch_growth_norms_2020
```

## Format

- measurementscale:

  measurementscale

- start_grade:

  start grade level

- end_grade:

  end grade level

- start_fallwinterspring:

  start season

- end_fallwinterspring:

  end season

- start_rit:

  starting RIT score

- typical_cohort_growth:

  expected mean growth for cohorts at this starting RIT

- sd_of_expectation:

  standard deviation of expected growth

## Source

NWEA 2020 MAP Growth Normative Data, Appendix E
