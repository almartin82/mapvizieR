# school growth norms 2015 study

school / cohort growth norms published by NWEA

## Usage

``` r
sch_growth_norms_2015
```

## Format

- end_grade:

  END grade, eg grade 2 spring to spring is grade 1 TO grade 2

- measurementscale:

  measurementscale

- growth_window:

  growth_window

- start_fallwinterspring:

  start_fallwinterspring

- end_fallwinterspring:

  end_fallwinterspring

- typical_cohort_growth:

  typical_cohort_growth

- sd_of_expectation:

  sd_of_expectation

- reported_cohort_growth:

  reported_cohort_growth

## Source

NWEA 2015 norms table, cleaned with 'growth norms munging.R' in
/data-raw
